Music

I like music because it connects to every part of my life. No matter what I’m doing—having fun, working, studying, or just relaxing—music is always there. It helps me express how I feel, even when I can’t find the right words. Some songs make me happy, some make me think, and others just let me feel everything at once. I love that there are so many kinds of music to fit every mood and moment. It’s not just something I listen to—it’s something that keeps me calm, inspired, and alive. Music feels like my way of expressing myself and understanding the world around me.

Shopping

I’ve always loved shopping—it’s one of my favorite things to do! I especially enjoy picking out clothes for myself, and I have a soft spot for headphones. But what I really love is finding gifts for the people I care about, like my sister, my friends, and even my dog. Giving presents just makes me happy! For my friend’s birthday, for example, I got her a bouquet of flowers and some toys of her favorite character, Strawberry Shortcake. Seeing someone’s face light up when they get a gift from me is honestly the best feeling.

Coloring

I’ve always loved coloring—it’s something that’s just really fun and a great way to pass the time. It’s also pretty relaxing. I used to have a lot of coloring books, like princess coloring books and My Little Pony ones, and I even enjoyed coloring on my phone. I especially like coloring cute things, nothing too crazy or super detailed.
